About The Band!

Beyond California are a 3-piece American band from the Fresno/Madera area. Formed in 2019, this group of multi-instrumentalists have been tearing up the Central Valley with their signature blend of grunge and punk. Make sure to catch one of their local shows and keep up with the band for the drop of their next album!



Members:

Marc Manzano - Vocals, Guitar, Bass
Ethan Stutsman - Vocals, Guitar, Bass
Andrew Avila - Drums
